Application Details

School fees are set at annual public meetings of School Governing Bodies (SGBs), where parents vote on the amount to be paid. At the Annual General and Budget Meeting held on 27 October 2022, a resolution for the compulsory school fees for the 2023 academic year was adopted

APPLICATIONS

  • The application process for the following year usually opens in about April/May of the previous year, but is dependent on the Department of Education finalizing the date every year.
  • The school has an Open Day which is when application forms are handed out and parents and prospective learners come and visit the school to see the facilities.
  • Applications are done manually and certified copies of documents are required (Unabridged Birth Certificate of the learner, ID copies of both parents, reports, proof of residence, etc.) No online applications are accepted.
