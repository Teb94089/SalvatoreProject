Academics

The academics at Salvatore Boarding School for the Young & Gifted are characterized by a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum, tailored to nurture and challenge the unique abilities of gifted students in a supernatural setting. The school places a strong emphasis on fostering intellectual growth, critical thinking, and creativity. Here's an overview of the academics:

Academics Faculties

ART COMPLEX

An art complex which houses two art rooms and a sculpture studio.

COMPUTER CENTRES

Three computer centres and WiFi access throughout the school and boarding establishment.

PERFORMING ARTS

A dedicated centre for the performing arts, including a drama practical room and music teaching and practical rooms.

PROGRAMMING

An excellent, vibrant media centre.

MEDIA CENTRE

An excellent, vibrant media centre..

SCIENCE LABORATORIES

Fully-equipped science laboratories.

SMART BOARDS

Interactive smart boards in classrooms.

CONSUMER STUDIES

Two Consumer Studies kitchens.

DIGITAL LEARNING

Access to Chromebooks in every classroom.
