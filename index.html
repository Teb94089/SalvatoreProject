WHO WE ARE AND WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

At Salvatore High School offers learners from Grade 8 to Grade 12 a dynamic learning Environment, with diverse opportunities for development for each individual

  • SHS is a proudly South African school.
  • It is led by highly-qualified and dedicated educators who guide our learners
  • in maintaining a balance between their academic, sporting and cultural endeavours.

OUR PLEDGE TO THE COMMUNITY

We provide a full educational service to our learners based on the pillars of knowledge and virtue embodied in sound moral and ethical standards. We will achieve this by a commitment to building a caring involved and creative community of learners staff and parents. Recognising and developing the unique potential of each individual. Creating and exploring a challenging and dynamic learning environment. Engendering a balance between the rights and responsibilities of the individual and the well being of the school the community and the natural environment. Equipping learners to participate effectively in a common future with all South Africans.
